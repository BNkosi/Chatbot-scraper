Overview
Move towards understanding utilizing Amazon's Web Services
Gain a high level overview of Amazon Web Services, and prepare to achieve AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner certification: a recommended, optional step toward achieving an Associate-level or Specialty certification.
Become Cloud ready
The course is built around learning and applying techniques which will enable you and your organisation to not only live, but thrive in the Cloud. 
Apply the skills
EXPLORE prides itself on the practicality of the course. Participants will be able to apply the knowledge that they learn with staff-facilitated labs and regular exercises on cloud-based infrastructure. 
Prepare to become certified
Completion of the course will fully prepare candidates to take the AWS Cloud Practitioner Certification Examination. Participants will have extended access to the material that are covered in the course, allowing them to revisit the techniques to keep it fresh in their mind. 
Work with the Best
Our support team have been there and done that - they are experienced practitioners of data science in various fields such as Utilities, Retail and Financial Services; with a solid academic background.
Curriculum
Start thinking in The Cloud
This course will provide individuals with the knowledge, skills, and experience required to complete the AWS Cloud Practitioner Certification Examination. You will learn how to practically instantiate end-to-end technological solutions in the AWS cloud utilising a multitude of compute and storage services, as well as the means to monitor, secure, and scale such solutions.
Understand Cloud terminology
Understand economic drivers for the Cloud and cost optimisation 
Understand AWS global infrastructure.
Look at AWS services: EC2, EBS, EFS, S3, RDS, DynamoDB, VPC.
Purpose and configuration of AWS services
Look at price of AWS services
Look at the five pillars of the AWS Well Architected Framework
Access control: security practices, AWS Policies and IAM Services
AWS guiding principles of scalability, reliability and availability 
Consolidated billing and volume-based discounts
AWS cost visualisation
Understand available support services and offerings  Overview
